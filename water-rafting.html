White Water Rafting – Kitulgala

Thrilling rapids in lush mid-country rainforest.

Rafting Overview

The Kelani River at Kitulgala offers fun, bouncy Class II–III rapids ideal for families, corporate groups and first-time rafters, with higher adrenaline lines after monsoon rains.

Rapids (Typical Run)

  1. Head Chopper
  2. Virgin's Breast
  3. Butter Crunch
  4. Killer Fall (portage at high water)
  5. Rafter's Retreat

Best Season

Year-round. Higher water May – Jun & Oct – Dec. Clearer, lower water Jan – Mar good for beginners.

Safety & Gear

  • Certified helmets, PFDs & paddles provided.
  • Professional river guides & safety kayaker (on higher flows).
  • Wear secure footwear (no flip-flops) & quick-dry clothing.

Other Activities

Canyoning, rainforest hikes, waterfall abseiling, bird watching (endemic Sri Lanka Grey Hornbill), and locations from the film 'The Bridge on the River Kwai'.

At A Glance

  • Region: Sabaragamuwa Province
  • Drive Time: ~2.5h from Colombo
  • River Grade: Class II–III
  • Trip Length: 5–7 km (60–90 min)
  • Min Age: 10+ (depends on water level)
